Our Mission

Christ's Mission Church - Mizpah is called to watch, expand the Kingdom, exercise spiritual dominion, and occupy faithfully until Jesus returns.

Matthew 26:41

To Watch

To remain alert in prayer, spiritual discernment, and faithful obedience.

Matthew 28:18-20

Expand the Kingdom

To preach, disciple, baptize, and teach the whole counsel of Jesus Christ.

Mark 16:14-18

Exercise Spiritual Dominion

To minister in faith, prayer, signs, and the authority of Christ.

Luke 19:13

Occupy Until Jesus Returns

To serve, steward, build, and remain fruitful until the Lord returns.
