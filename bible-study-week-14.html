God's Kingdom Laws: Loving God - No Idolatry

A key requirement to receiving God's Kingdom promises is to love God, our heavenly Father, our El Elyon, the Great Most High. To love God is to obey and keep His commandments. His commandments are not grievous.

In this study, we shall study how to truly love God through rejecting all kinds of idolatrous and false worship. God seeks those who worship Him in spirit and truth. To love God and worship Him alone is key. There are many things that men worship instead of the true God. Idolatry is forbidden.

Opening Scripture 1 John 5:2-3
NIV MEV NKJV
Monday, June 22

Do Not Worship Created Things in Heaven or on Earth

God has forbidden the worship of any created thing in heaven or on earth. In the heavens we have the planets, the sun, moon, and stars. On earth, we have man, trees, plants, stones, rivers, mountains, beasts, birds, and crawling things. He has also forbidden directly worshipping these things or making images of such things and bowing down to them. We must love God with all our heart, soul, and mind.

Do not worship idols Exodus 20:1-7; Jeremiah 10:1-16; Ezekiel 8:14-16; Acts 17:16-31; Romans 1:18-25; 1 John 5:21
NIV MEV NKJV
Do not worship money and wealth Psalm 49:6-9; Psalm 115:2-8; Psalm 135:15-18; Proverbs 11:4; Isaiah 2:20-21; Isaiah 46:5-7; Ezekiel 7:19; Daniel 5:23; Matthew 6:24; Revelation 9:20
NIV MEV NKJV
Tuesday, June 23

Do Not Worship Angels and Demons

Satan and his fallen angels called demons love to be worshipped. True angels refuse to be worshipped. Worshipping of angels is forbidden.

Do not worship Satan Matthew 4:8-11; John 8:31-47
NIV MEV NKJV
Do not worship demons Deuteronomy 32:15-25; Psalm 82:1-8; Psalm 96:4-6; 1 Corinthians 10:14-22; Revelation 9:20
NIV MEV NKJV
Do not worship angels Colossians 2:18; Hebrews 1:13-14; Revelation 19:10
NIV MEV NKJV
Thursday, June 25

Do Not Worship Spirits

There are very many types of evil and wicked spirits in the world. These spirits can be summoned by witches, diviners, and sorcerers through enchantments and various sacrifices to manipulate situations, lie, perform miracles, or prophesy.

The good spirits are only released by the Holy Spirit in the form of gifts of the Holy Spirit.

Do not worship bewitching or cursing spirits Leviticus 19:31; Leviticus 20:6, 27
Do not worship sorcery, magic, or demonic miracle spirits Deuteronomy 18:9-14; Acts 8:9-11; Acts 13:6
Do not worship lying or deceiving spirits 1 Kings 22:19-23; 1 Timothy 4:1
Do not worship divining or demonic prophecy spirits Genesis 30:27; Acts 16:16-18
Do not worship familiar spirits or spirits of the dead 1 Samuel 28:8-14; Isaiah 8:19-22
Friday, June 26

Do Not Worship Men or Animal Power

Men also like to worship themselves, other men, the work of their hands, abilities, and animal power. We must not do this.

Do not worship yourself Proverbs 3:5-7; Proverbs 16:5; Proverbs 27:2; Proverbs 28:26; Daniel 4:28-37; Luke 14:11
Do not worship powerful men Psalm 60:11; Psalm 146:3-10; Jeremiah 17:5-9; Daniel 3:8-18; Mark 7:8-9; Acts 12:20-24
Do not worship powerful beasts and chariots Psalm 20:7-8; Psalm 33:17-19; Psalm 147:10-11; Isaiah 31:1-3
