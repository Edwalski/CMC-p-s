On Pentecost the Holy Spirit came with all the anointing power for the church. However, the power of the Holy Spirit is not automatic for the believer. For the Holy Spirit to release miracle working power, He has to be fanned into flame. To do this, we need to deploy spiritual keys to fan the power of the Spirit for the miraculous to happen.

Monday, May 25

Key #1: Be in One Accord in Prayer

It is absolutely important that believers gather together in unity to fellowship and pray. Power is only given to those in unity of fellowship. No unity, no love, no power. You cannot walk in power if you are a lone ranger. Stay-at-home believers cannot exercise spiritual power. Being too busy to be with the church is a sign of no power.

Scriptures Psalms 133; John 17:20-23; Acts 1:4-8, 12-14; Acts 2:1-4; Romans 12:9-16; Ephesians 4:1-8; Philippians 2:1-6; Hebrews 10:25
Tuesday, May 26

Key #2: Laying on of Hands

The frequent laying on of hands by mature ministers on those in regular fellowship and unity with the church grants access to the power of the Holy Spirit.

Scriptures Deuteronomy 34:9; Acts 8:14-17; Acts 19:1-7; 1 Timothy 4:14; 2 Timothy 1:6
Thursday, May 28

Key #3: Prophetic Praise, Prophetic Tongues & Prophesy

The Holy Spirit loves an atmosphere of prophetic praise and worship. In this atmosphere, praise God with all kinds of tongues. Praise God with your native tongue and other unknown tongues. Start with tongues you know and understand and grow. Prophetic worship and tongues exalt God on what He has done. After the tongues, begin to prophesy. To prophesy is to give assignment to the Holy Spirit on what to do. Speak, declare and decree things in the atmosphere of worship. You will be amazed at the miracles that will come your way. Be your own prophet. You know your mountains and valleys much better than anyone else.

Prophetic Praise Exodus 15:20; 1 Samuel 10:5-6; 1 Chronicles 25:1; 2 Chronicles 20:21-22; Ephesians 5:18-20
Prophetic Tongues Acts 2:5-12; Acts 10:44-46; 1 Corinthians 14:1
Prophesy 2 Kings 3:14-22; Job 22:27-28
Friday, May 29

Key #4: Use of Prophetic Materials

Material things can be used to arouse the power of the Holy Spirit to act. Things in nature have ears and voices; they speak to the spiritual world. When subjected to God, they are used by the Holy Spirit as signs to activate heavenly agenda. You need to discern which material to use, when, how and where. Never use these materials prophetically if you are not fully immersed in Christ. Remember the sons of Sceva. I will share about 13 prophetic materials that activate spiritual power.

Prophetic Materials 1. Sacrifice - Genesis 15:7-10; 2. Food - Genesis 31:51-55; 3. Wine - Genesis 35:14-15; 4. Sticks - Exodus 15:23-25; 5. Fire - Deuteronomy 7:5-6; 6. Water - 1 Samuel 7:5-6; 7. Stones - 1 Samuel 7:12-13; 8. Salt - 2 Kings 2:19-22; 9. Clothes - 2 Kings 2:8,13-14; 10. Tools - 2 Kings 13:14-19; 11. Anointing oil - Mark 6:12-13; 12. Saliva - Mark 8:22-26; 13. Soil - John 9:6-7
