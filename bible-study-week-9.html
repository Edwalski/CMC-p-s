Faith Actions That Activate Heavenly Response After Prayer

Monday, May 18 - Saturday, May 23, 2026

When you pray, angels collect the prayers from the Holy Spirit and offer them to the four living creatures and the twenty four elders who sit around the throne of God. In turn, the four living creatures and the twenty four elders offer the prayers of the saints to the Father and Jesus Christ. After these offerings of prayer, the Holy Spirit and the angels responsible for human affairs are authorized to take action. The hand of God on earth is activated through the Holy Spirit and His holy angels.

If we do not pray, the Holy Spirit and angels are idle, the four living creatures and the twenty four elders have nothing to offer at the throne of God. See Revelation 5:8-10 and Revelation 8:3-5. Without prayer, heaven cannot help earth. Human prayer is the energy and license that authorizes heaven to move on earth.

However, for the Holy Spirit and angels to produce answers and miracles on earth, there are faith actions that those who pray must perform after prayer, for heaven to act. That is to say that for heaven to act, we need prayer plus actions of faith. Faith without action is dead. Prayer without faith actions is dead.

It is important to understand that prayer is directed to heaven to authorize heaven to act on a situation. But a faith action is directed at the object needing mercy by another human being, so that heaven can act upon that situation. After prayer, heaven always waits for a human action of faith before it can respond. Many prayers are never answered because we are afraid to carry out actions of faith after prayer.

You must discern each situation and get revelation on what faith action is needed in what situation. If one is full of prayer and fasting, the Holy Spirit will reveal what faith action is needed. Remember, anyone who is full of prayer and fasting can perform faith actions. It is not for ministers alone, but for the saints.

Monday, May 18

Faith Action #1: Bless, Lay Hands, Anoint With Oil

To produce miracle power from the Holy Spirit and angels, it is important to move by actions of faith and bless, lay hands and anoint with oil the person or object upon which we need heaven to act after prayer. These can be done for people, animals, land, trees and objects. Bless, lay hands, and anoint with oil in the name of Jesus through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Scriptures Matthew 14:23-27; Mark 1:35-42; John 9:1-12; Acts 3:1-8; Acts 5:12-16; Acts 9:36-42; Romans 12:14; James 2:14-17; James 5:13-18
Tuesday, May 19

Faith Action #2: Command, Cast Out & Rebuke Evil Spirits & Demons

The second faith action is by commanding demons and evil spirits to be cast out from people, animals, places, objects and other situations in the name of Jesus by the power of the Holy Spirit. When engaging demons and wicked spirits, one must be full of prayer and fasting.

Scriptures Matthew 17:1-21; Mark 4:35-41; Mark 6:12-13; Luke 4:31-37; Acts 8:6-8; Acts 13:6-12; Acts 19:11-12
Thursday, May 21

Faith Action #3: Prophesy, Decree, Declare & Do Prophetic Actions

The third faith action is to prophesy, do prophetic actions, decree and declare logos and rhema revelations that the Holy Spirit puts in your heart and mouth. The best moments to prophesy and decree prophetic declarations and actions is after an intense prayer, praise and worship session. Spiritual songs and hymns arouse the prophetic grace. Prophesy boldly. Speak prophetically to the person or thing needing the prophetic power of the Holy Spirit. Some things will look unusual when you act prophetically.

Scriptures 1 Samuel 10:1-11; 2 Kings 2:19-25; 2 Kings 3:14-19; 2 Kings 6:1-7; 1 Chronicles 25:1-7; Job 22:27-28; Psalms 23:5-6; Psalms 92:13-15; Proverbs 10:22; Isaiah 54:17; Matthew 21:12-22; Mark 8:22-26; Romans 8:28, 37-39
