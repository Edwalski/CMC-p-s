Week 9 Bible Study | Monday, May 18 - Saturday, May 23, 2026

Faith Actions That Activate Heavenly Response After Prayer

Year 5787 | 2-6 Sivan | 3rd Month

This week's teaching explores how prayer authorizes heaven to act on earth, and how believers must carry out faith actions after prayer.

Full notes include the teaching introduction, daily comments, scriptures, and Pentecost focus for the week.

Read Full Notes
Monday, May 18

Faith Action #1: Bless, Lay Hands, Anoint With Oil

Move by faith and bless, lay hands on, and anoint with oil the person, land, animal, tree, or object upon which heaven needs to act.

Tuesday, May 19

Faith Action #2: Command, Cast Out & Rebuke Evil Spirits

Command demons and evil spirits to be cast out from people, animals, places, and objects in the name of Jesus by the power of the Holy Spirit.

Thursday, May 21

Faith Action #3: Prophesy, Decree, Declare & Do Prophetic Actions

Prophesy, decree, declare, and act on the logos and rhema revelations the Holy Spirit puts in your heart and mouth after prayer, praise, and worship.
