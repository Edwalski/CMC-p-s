About Christ's Mission Church

A people of faith, confession, prayer, and transformation through the Heavenly Father, Jesus Christ, and the Holy Spirit.

1 Samuel 7:3-17

We Are the Mizpah People

Mizpah in Hebrew means to watch. We are commanded by Jesus Christ to watch and pray (Matthew 26:41). At Mizpah, Prophet Samuel called Israel to repentance from serving the Baals. The first message of Jesus was to call Israel to repentance and faith unto God (Matthew 4:17). At Mizpah, Israel tarried long in prayer and fasting in order to defeat the powers against them. Paul commands the church to pray with all kinds of prayer in the Holy Ghost to overcome wicked principalities (Ephesians 6:10-18).

At Mizpah, Samuel taught and judged the people to follow God's ways. Jesus commanded all who believe, repent and are baptized to be taught the whole counsel of God (Matthew 28:20). At Mizpah, Samuel and the people gave sacrifices and offerings to God. Paul commands the church to excel in the grace of giving (2 Corinthians 8:7). At Mizpah, Samuel performed prophetic signs. Paul desires that all believers prophesy (1 Corinthians 14:1).

At Mizpah, the Philistines were uprooted and fled. Jesus Christ gave us power over the spirits and powers of darkness (Luke 10:17-19). At Mizpah, Israel was restored to her land and cities. Jesus Christ promised that in this life, we shall receive land, houses and family (Mark 10:28-30). From Mizpah, Samuel expanded his apostolic ministry to other towns in Israel. We are called to take over our immediate territory for the Gospel and expand to other cities (Acts 1:8).

That is why we call ourselves the Mizpah people.

Statement of Faith

What We Believe

1

We believe that to enter the Kingdom of God and be part of God's church on earth, one has to be born again by believing the gospel message of Jesus Christ and of the Kingdom of God, be baptized in water by immersion, and receive the Holy Spirit by the laying on of hands.

2

We believe that the church of Jesus Christ is a diverse living organism of different denominations and groupings, called from believing Jews and believing Gentiles to become a separate nation on earth as God's instrument for the gospel message, spiritual light, power and dominion in this present age against unbelief, sin, evil and the kingdom of darkness.

3

We believe that at Passover and from time to time, all believers must partake of the Lord's Table of unleavened bread, the cup of wine and the washing of feet, as symbols of the New Covenant of the suffering, death, burial, resurrection and return of Christ.

4

We believe the Hebrew books from Genesis to Malachi, the Gospels from Matthew to Acts, and the apostolic letters of the original apostles of Jesus Christ are the authentic Scripture and written Word of God for Israel, the church and all humanity, on which to base cultures, customs, traditions, practices, teachings and standards of living that please God.

5

We believe and keep the feasts and celebrations of God: Passover, Unleavened Bread, Firstfruits, Pentecost, Trumpets, Atonement, Tabernacles, Dedication and Purim, as prophetic and spiritual markers of God's seasons.

6

We believe in the second coming of Jesus Christ at a time no one knows, to cast the devil and his angels into the bottomless pit, resurrect and reward the righteous in the first resurrection, and set up His kingdom on earth for 1000 years, to rule over humanity left on earth at His second coming.

7

We believe that after the 1000 years, Satan and his angels will be released and later cast into the lake of fire. The second resurrection and great white throne judgment will take place, and God will make a new heavens and a new earth for eternity with Him, Christ and His chosen bride, the resurrected church.

Our Confession

The Usual Is Over

Our faith in the Heavenly Father, Jesus Christ, and the Holy Spirit is alive in this moment. By faith, we declare that we will never be the same again.

Heavenly Father

We Are Children of the Father

Having received the Father's love in Christ Jesus, we confess that He is our Father and we are His children, born of water and by the Spirit. In Him, we live in His will, His benefits, and Abraham's blessing.

Jesus Christ

We Are Redeemed in Christ

Having received Jesus Christ as King, Lord, and Savior, we confess that we are redeemed from every curse. By His blood, we overcome darkness and proclaim His great name to all people.

Holy Spirit

We Walk in the Spirit's Power

Having received the Holy Spirit from Jesus Christ, we confess that His anointing abides in us. By His power, we stand in prayer, fasting, revelation, service, and fellowship to grow the body of Christ.

Therefore, with the help of the Holy Spirit, we keep standing.

We stand on God's Word and His promises. What the Father has spoken over our lives through Jesus Christ, the Holy Spirit will bring to pass.
